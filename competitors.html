Confirmed Weaknesses & Ammo

  • LOYALTY IS BROKEN: Points redemption threshold is so high that customers can never realistically cash out. It doesn't drive repeat visits.

    • Restaurant closes at 9pm → orders still accepted at 9:09pm (kitchen already closed)

  • SEO MANIPULATION: They dominate their own search results — almost zero independent negative reviews surface publicly. This is deliberate. Real intel lives in restaurant owner networks.
  • No true restaurant benchmarking — can't compare your performance to similar restaurants in market
  • Hardware is standard iPad, not purpose-built for restaurant environment (spills, drops)
  • No native payroll, no native scheduling — third-party only
